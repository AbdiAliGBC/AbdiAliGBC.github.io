Capstone Project : Stray No More App

The Stray No More App is the app my group and I decided on for our final
capstone project. It is an app that simulates a community for pet owners,
but mainly is for people who have lost their pets in the city and people who
come across stray animals. Information is given to users on how to handle stray
animals. Most importantly, users are able to create a profile for themselves with
a picture of their pet, and are able to post messages on forums with other users.
We are currently in the implementation stage of the project, with some of the
functionality complete.
